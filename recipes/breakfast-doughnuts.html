High Protein Cookie Dough Balls

Perfect for the rushers. These cookie dough balls are packed with protein and will satisfy your sweet tooth while giving a boost of energy.

The go-to meal before a tough morning workout since the carbs will get you through. Feel free to swap out or add any extras to satisfy your macros

Ingredients

  • 100g of Oats
  • 30g Sukrin or Dates
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 60g Egg Whites (40mL Liquid)
  • 30g Powdered Peanut Butter

Add Ons

  • Cacao Nibs/Chocolate
  • Fruit (dry of fresh)
  • Chopped Nuts
  • Soya Crispies
  • Chopped Protein Bar

Instructions

  1. Mix all dry incredients together and blend them
  2. Add in any chosen 'Add Ons'
  3. Add in egg whites and mix well
  4. For a thinner consistency, add more egg whites

Nutrition/serving

  • 575kcal
  • 39g Protein
  • 71g Carbs
  • 10g Fat
