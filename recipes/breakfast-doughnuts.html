Breakfast Doughnuts

It's true! You can have doughnuts for breakfast

The perfect breakfast for when you are on the go. And it goes great with coffee!

A high protein, low carb breakfast treat. Recipe makes 6 donuts.

Ingredients

  • 130g Egg Whites
  • 30mL Milk (of choice)
  • 1 1/2tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Scoop Protein Powder (Vanilla)
  • 1tsp Baking powder
  • 36g Oat Flour

Add Ons

  • Chia Seed Jam
  • Powdered Peanut Butter Sauce
  • Berries
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Sooya Crispies

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl
  3. Lightly grease a non-stick doughnut tin and spoon mixture into it
  4. Cook for 6-8 minutes
  5. Take out of oven and top with your chosen toppings. Enjoy!

Nutrition/doughnut

  • 58kcal
  • 7g Protein
  • 4g Carbs
  • 0.6g Fat
