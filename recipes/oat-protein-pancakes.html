Oat Protein Pancakes

High Protein Oat Protein Pancakes

For the weekends or if you want to wake up a bit earlier to enjoy these. Packed with protein and so tasty. Recipe servces 1 person.

Ingredients

  • 80g of Oats
  • 40g Protein Powder
  • 100g Egg Whites
  • 250mL Almond Milk

Add Ons

  • 2tbsp Peanut Butter Sauce
  • Maple Syrup
  • Sliced Fruits/Berries

Instructions

  1. Blend the oats into a flour
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and blend further to make a smooth batter
  3. Heat a pan to medium heat and lightly grease with a little oil
  4. Add the mix to each corner of the pan to form little circles
  5. Add the mix to each corner of the pan to form little circles
  6. Cook until bubbles form on top and then flip until both sides are brown
  7. service with your choice of toppings - they're unlimited to the Add Ons we suggested!

Nutrition/serving

  • 670kcal
  • 45g Protein
  • 65g Carbs
  • 22g Fat
